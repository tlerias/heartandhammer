The Vision and Mission of Heart & Hammer started much before a name or website was ever created. We began as a family with the opportunity to make a positive impact in the communities where we grew up. Both domestically and abroad, our families have donated time, energy, goods, and money to help those in need. We are committed to doing what we can with the connections and resources we have and are excited to begin this journey with the Home-Reach Foundation 501(c)(3). Home-Reach is dedicated to providing medical care and socio-economic assistance particularly to the poor in the Philippines. Home-Reach Foundation supports Heart & Hammer to accomplish its mission; Health, Education, Livelihood and Patrimony (HELP).

Our Coordinators
Tara Lerias

Born and raised in New York, Tara Lerias is currently a Front End Developer for Priceline.com. Tara was just 16 years old when she participated in her first humanitarian mission and traveled to Bohol, Philippines in 2007. “The most cultural shocking moment of my life was getting off the bus for the first time and seeing thirty or so kids playing on the side of the road with cans.They had no shoes, no clothes, and their homes were made of old store front signs and palm leaves.” From this experience, Tara has been inspired to continue helping those in need. Tara is a founding member of Heart and Hammer, and the website developer for the organization.

Rachel Ruggieri
Tyler Ruggieri

Rachel and Tyler Ruggieri both currently have established careers in tech startups. Rachel has a focus on the event industry while Tyler has extensive experience in sales. Over the years, they have contributed to the Philippine missions by collecting donations in the states. Rachel first took the trip in 2012 and Tyler joined the next year in 2013. The experiences inspired the two to be as involved as possible moving forward with the hope of making great impact in others lives. If each person took care of their fellow man, we would never be in need.

Gabriella Coradini

Gabriella Coradini is a dedicated Heart and Hammer volunteer. Born in the Philippines herself, Gabriella attended her first humanitarian mission in 2007 to Bohol, Philippines in hopes of giving back to her home country. What keeps her coming back to the Philippines on these missions are the smiling faces she receives from all types of people being helped by the care and support, especially the children of these families in need. Gabriella also took a year out of her college in 2014/15 to go to Bolhol and volunteer schooling children with special needs. Gabriella is a proud member of the Heart and Hammer family in hopes of continuing to bring smiles to the people in need of help all over the Philippines.

Daniella Coradini

Daniella Coradini is a long time Heart and Hammer volunteer. Born in the Philippines as the twin sister to Gabriella, Daniella has been a regular on multiple missions to the Philippines since 2007 providing front and back room support as well as fund-raising efforts in middle & high school. Together with her twin sister, Gabriella, she strives to bring hope and encouragement to those in need by letting them know someone cares.

Maria Coradini
Robert Coradini

Robert and Maria Coradini are one of the leading Heart and Hammer volunteers through their generosity & contributions to the numerous humanitarian missions. Robert, as a former J&J Senior Executive and now President of Oak Tree Advisors LLC., along with his wife Maria, have been active in raising donations as well as shipping care packages to those in need. Norminda (Northern Mindanao Assoc.) recognized them in 2007 in appreciation of their support. In 2012, the Coradini Family, including Daniella & Gabriella, received the Humanitarian of the Year award by PMAA (Philippine Medical Association in America) for their contributions, dedication and hard work during the medical mission to Cebu City earlier that year. They, along with their daughters, remain committed to sharing their time, talent & treasures in bringing care & support to those in need.

Edgar Lerias

Dr. Edgar Lerias is a long-standing community leader, who owns two Pediatric Medical offices in Deer Park and Farmingdale, Long Island, NY and he is also a Pediatric Attending Physician Consultant in St Francis Hospital, Roslyn, NY Edgar's passion is to give freely of his time to help underprivileged and depressed communities in the Philippines and New York, by coordinating and volunteering various medical/surgical /humanitarian efforts. In the last few years, for instance, Edgar coordinated a surgery/medical/dental/humanitarian missions in Camarines Sur, Ilocos Sur and Cebu City, Philippines. He volunteers annually to Suffolk County Asian American Board in partnership with Tzu Chi International Medical Association of Long Island which provide charity health fairs in Long Island and Queens. Dr. Lerias is member of the American Pediatric Society; member of the Board of Directors for Home-Reach Foundation; past President of the Philippine Medical Association in America; past President of the Philippine Physicians of New York, past President of the Northern Mindano Association of Eastern Seaboard; and past President of the Fil-Am Golfer’s Association
